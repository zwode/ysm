Services

Tui na

Tui Na, which translates to "push and grasp," is an integral part of Traditional Chinese Medicine. This therapeutic massage technique focuses on balancing the body's vital energy, known as "Qi," by stimulating specific acupressure points and meridians. Through rhythmic movements and precise pressure, Tui Na promotes the flow of Qi, releases tension, and restores harmony within.

Deep Tissue

Deep Tissue Massage is a therapeutic technique designed to release chronic patterns of tension lurking within your muscles and connective tissues. Through slow, deliberate strokes and concentrated pressure, our skilled therapists access deep layers of muscle fibers, alleviating discomfort and promoting healing from within.
